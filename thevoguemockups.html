FIGMA MOCKUPS
These are a series of page mockups I created in Figma prior to coding the actual site. Some of them were translated fairly 1:1, others were changed drastically or even omitted. You can click the photos to view them individually, and click the titles of each section to navigate to their final version!
Homepage
I liked the idea of having a hero image that was interacting with the title of the site, and ended up implementing a different one on every page in the coded version. I filled the movie section up with films I like, and it plays to the idea that the actual Vogue theater screened primarily niche films.
About Us
I wanted to have a page describing the theater and its history, but finding information on a theater that's been closed since 1998 proved difficult! I ended up using a different image for the final site and adding a section about their 'Rocky Horror Picture Show' events.
Member's Club
I got the idea for the Member's Page from researching other movie websites. Having some kind of member's program seems to be a pretty common thing when it comes to other theater chains, so I wanted to do one of my own. I tried to make the pricing and perks seem competitive compared to what most national chains are offering.
Coming Soon
This page was a 'coming soon' for what the theater would be offering in future showings. However, I ended up replacing it with a concessions page in the final version because the similarities between the 'coming soon' page and the homepage were a bit too similar.
Reservations
The final page was for people who might want to rent a private theater viewing. Every webpage needed some a form section on it as a requirement, so I did my best to do something a bit more interesting than just a 'contact us' page for the site.
