ABOUT US

The Vogue was opened on December 22, 1939 in Louisville Kentucky, and has been in operation for 86 years! We are a small independently owned and operated theater, specializing in re-showings of cult classic films. We offer a wide variety of concessions, a Member's Club, and even private showings!

EVENTS

Every Saturday at Midnight, come and participate in an interactive viewing of the Rocky Horror Picture Show! Guests are encouraged to dress up, and prop bags for audience participation will be available in the lobby before the show.
